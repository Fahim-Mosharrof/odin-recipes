Pizza

Description

Pizza is a beloved Italian dish made from a flattened dough base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings, then baked until the crust is crispy and the cheese is melted. It comes in many varieties, from classic Margherita to loaded meat and veggie options.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh basil

Preparation

  1. Prepare Dough: Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness, then transfer it to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  2. Add Sauce: Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges of the crust.
  3. Top with Cheese: Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
  4. Add Toppings: Drizzle a bit of olive oil and add fresh basil leaves or any other desired toppings (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, olives).
  5. Bake: Preheat the oven to its highest setting (usually around 475-500°F or 245-260°C), then bake the pizza for 10-15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  6. Serve: Remove the pizza from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, then slice and serve hot.
